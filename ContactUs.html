Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start donating clothes?

Simply create an account, list your items with photos and descriptions, and we'll help connect you with charities in your area.

What types of clothing do you accept?

We accept all types of clean, wearable clothing in good condition. This includes shirts, pants, dresses, jackets, and accessories.

How does the AI categorization work?

Our AI analyzes photos of your donated items to automatically categorize them by type, season, and condition, making the process faster for charities.

Can I track my environmental impact?

Yes! Every donor has a dashboard that shows their CO₂ savings, landfill reduction, and the number of people helped through their donations.
